## FDA Actual: Airsupra [protocol-design] — Round 04

> Source: NDA214070 MultidisciplineR.md (MANDALA + DENALI)

### 1. design_type
**TWO pivotal trials:**
- MANDALA: Event-driven, randomized, double-blind, comparator-controlled, PRN use
- DENALI: 12-week, randomized, double-blind, placebo- and comparator-controlled, QID fixed-dose

### 2. control_arm
- MANDALA: Albuterol sulfate (AS) PRN (active comparator, not placebo)
- DENALI: 5-arm — BDA 160/180, BDA 80/180, budesonide alone, albuterol alone, placebo (factorial)

### 3. primary_endpoint
- MANDALA: Time to first severe asthma exacerbation
- DENALI: Dual primary — (a) FEV1 AUC0-6h over 12 weeks + (b) trough FEV1 at week 12

### 4. endpoint_definition
- MANDALA: Severe exacerbation = OCS ≥3 days OR hospitalization OR ED visit for asthma; efficacy estimand
- DENALI: Change from baseline in FEV1 AUC0-6h + change from baseline in trough FEV1; mITT

### 5. secondary_endpoints
- MANDALA key secondary (hierarchical): annualized rate of severe exacerbations, total SCS use
- DENALI secondary: time to onset/duration of response (15% FEV1 increase) on Day 1, ACQ-7, trough FEV1 at week 1

### 6. sample_size
- MANDALA: N=3,132 randomized (moderate-to-severe, ≥4 years, 296 centers, 11 countries)
- DENALI: N=1,001 randomized (mild asthma, 5-arm ~200/arm, 110 centers)

### 7. stat_framework
- MANDALA: Cox PH model, hierarchical testing for key secondaries
- DENALI: Dual primary (co-primary), IUT convention expected; combination rule satisfaction
- Efficacy estimand + COVID-19 estimand (supportive)

### 8. stratification
- MANDALA: age (≥4-<12, ≥12-<18, ≥18; for adults: age ≥18 relevant) + region + exacerbation history (1 vs >1)
- DENALI: (not explicitly detailed in excerpt but per SOP: age + ACQ-7 + background ICS)

### 9. eligibility
- MANDALA: ≥4 years (adults ≥18 for approved indication), moderate-to-severe asthma, ≥1 severe exacerbation in prior 12 months
- DENALI: ≥4 years, mild asthma requiring PRN SABA alone or low-dose ICS + PRN SABA

### 10. operational
- MANDALA: 296 centers, 11 countries, PRN administration
- DENALI: 110 centers, QID fixed-dose, 5-arm factorial
- Dosing: BDA 160/180 (budesonide 160µg + albuterol 180µg per dose, 2 actuations)
