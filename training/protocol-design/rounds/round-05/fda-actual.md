## FDA Actual: Tavneos [protocol-design] — Round 05

> Source: NDA214487 MultidisciplineR.md (Study CL010_168 / ADVOCATE)

### 1. design_type
Randomized, double-blind, double-dummy, active-controlled, 52-week study. N=331 (1:1).

### 2. control_arm
Protocol-specified 20-week prednisone taper + background CYC or RTX (investigator choice). Double-dummy: both arms take matching capsules/tablets.

### 3. primary_endpoint
Co-primary: (1) Remission at Week 26 (BVAS=0, adjudicated); (2) Sustained remission at Week 52 (BVAS=0 at both Wk26 and Wk52).

### 4. endpoint_definition
BVAS=0 by Adjudication Committee (not investigator). NI at Week 26 → Superiority at Week 52 stepdown. Treatment difference with 95% CI. Met: NI at Wk26 (diff 3.4%, CI -6.0 to 12.8); Superiority at Wk52 (diff 12.5%, CI 2.6 to 22.3).

### 5. secondary_endpoints
GTI (Glucocorticoid Toxicity Index), eGFR, time to remission, BVAS relapse, renal disease subgroup.

### 6. sample_size
N=331 randomized (166 avacopan, 165 prednisone). 52 weeks.

### 7. stat_framework
NI→superiority stepdown at Wk26→Wk52. CMH analysis. Hierarchical testing. FDA had concerns about NI margin justification.

### 8. stratification
Disease status (newly diagnosed vs relapsing), ANCA type (PR3 vs MPO), background therapy (CYC vs RTX).

### 9. eligibility
Adults with newly diagnosed or relapsing GPA/MPA, PR3-ANCA or MPO-ANCA positive, active disease requiring CYC or RTX induction.

### 10. operational
Global multicenter. Avacopan 30mg BID (not TID). Prednisone taper: 60mg → 0 over 20 weeks. Double-dummy blinding. Adjudication committee for BVAS.
