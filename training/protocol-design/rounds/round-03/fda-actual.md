## FDA Actual: Vyloy [protocol-design] — Round 03

> Source: BLA761365 MultidisciplineR.md (SPOTLIGHT + GLOW)
> Note: FDA approved based on TWO independent pivotal trials (SPOTLIGHT + GLOW)

### 1. design_type
**Double-blind, placebo-controlled, multiregional, randomized (1:1), parallel-group Phase 3 trial**
- TWO independent trials: SPOTLIGHT (NCT03504397, mFOLFOX6 backbone) and GLOW (NCT03653507, CAPOX backbone)
- Both: 1:1 randomization, double-blind, placebo-controlled, add-on design

### 2. control_arm
**Placebo + fluoropyrimidine/platinum chemotherapy (NO immunotherapy)**
- SPOTLIGHT: Placebo + mFOLFOX6 (5-FU + leucovorin + oxaliplatin)
- GLOW: Placebo + CAPOX (capecitabine + oxaliplatin)
- After 12 cycles (SPOTLIGHT) or 8 cycles (GLOW) of oxaliplatin, allowed to continue with zolbetuximab/placebo + fluoropyrimidine at investigator discretion

### 3. primary_endpoint
**PFS per RECIST v1.1 as assessed by Independent Review Committee (IRC) in all randomized patients**
- SPOTLIGHT: HR 0.75 (95% CI 0.598-0.942, 1-sided p=0.0066), mPFS 10.6 vs 8.7 months
- GLOW: HR 0.687 (95% CI 0.544-0.866, 1-sided p=0.0007), mPFS 8.2 vs 6.8 months

### 4. endpoint_definition
- Time from randomization to first documented disease progression per RECIST v1.1 (IRC/BICR assessed) or death from any cause
- IRC assessment (not investigator-assessed)
- All randomized patients (FAS/ITT)

### 5. secondary_endpoints
**Key secondary (hierarchical testing order):**
1. OS — SPOTLIGHT: HR 0.75, mOS 18.2 vs 15.5 months; GLOW: HR 0.77, mOS 14.4 vs 12.2 months
2. TTCD (Time to Clinical Deterioration)

**Additional:**
- ORR
- DOR
- Other outcomes

### 6. sample_size
**SPOTLIGHT: N=565 (283:282)**
**GLOW: N=507 (254:253)**
**Combined: N=1,072**
- Two independent trials, each ~500-565 patients

### 7. stat_framework
- Hierarchical testing: PFS → OS → TTCD (fixed sequence)
- 1-sided p-values reported
- Pre-specified analyses of PFS and OS determined to be statistically significant in both trials
- Two independent trials provide confirmation

### 8. stratification
**3 factors (same in both trials):**
1. Region (Asia vs Non-Asia)
2. Number of organs with metastatic sites (0-2 vs ≥3)
3. Prior gastrectomy (Yes vs No)

### 9. eligibility
**Inclusion:**
- Previously untreated unresectable or metastatic gastric/GEJ adenocarcinoma
- HER2-negative
- CLDN18.2-positive: ≥75% of tumor cells with moderate-to-strong membranous CLDN18 staining (VENTANA CLDN18 43-14A RxDx Assay, central lab)

**Exclusion:**
- Complete or partial gastric outlet syndrome
- History of CNS metastases

### 10. operational
**Geography:** Multiregional (Asia, Europe, Americas)
**Biomarker testing:** Central lab IHC — VENTANA CLDN18 (43-14A) RxDx Assay (CDx)
**Dosing:** Zolbetuximab 800 mg/m² loading D1C1 → 600 mg/m² Q3W maintenance
**Chemo:** SPOTLIGHT: mFOLFOX6 (up to 12 cycles oxaliplatin); GLOW: CAPOX (up to 8 cycles oxaliplatin)
