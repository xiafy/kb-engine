## Trial Design

### Biomarker Testing Strategy
IF indication == "KRAS G12C tumor": → Dual companion diagnostics (tissue PCR + plasma ctDNA NGS), submit sPMA concurrently with NDA.

### Stratification Factors
NSCLC accelerated approval recommended stratification/subgroup factors: EGFR/ALK status + PD-L1 expression + prior lines of therapy.
