# Nasopharyngeal Carcinoma (NPC)

> version: 1.0 | source: sop.md v2.2 split

## Trial Design

### Stratification Factors
NPC recommended stratification factors: ECOG PS (0 vs 1) + disease stage (recurrent vs metastatic).
Note: Region is not stratified (all Asian).

### Safety Monitoring
IF indication == "NPC":
→ EBV viral load monitoring (baseline, Q2 cycles Year 1, Q3 cycles thereafter, at progression), used for subgroup analysis
